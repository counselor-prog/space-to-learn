The PAUSE practice

Five steps. Five minutes. A way to interrupt a spiral before it takes over. You can do this anywhere, anytime you feel overwhelmed, anxious, or stuck.

What is PAUSE?

P
Pause and breatheStop what you are doing. Take one slow, deliberate breath.
A
Ask yourselfWhat am I actually feeling right now? Name it without judging it.
U
Understand the choiceWhat is one small thing I can do, or let go of, right now?
S
Say your mantra"Thinking is the root cause of suffering." Let the thought be there without feeding it.
E
Experience your emotionsLet yourself feel what is there, without trying to fix it or push it away.
🌿

That was your PAUSE.

You just gave yourself five minutes instead of letting the spiral decide. That is not nothing. Come back to this any time you need it.
